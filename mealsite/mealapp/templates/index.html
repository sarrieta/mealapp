Our Story

Welcome

Mealdr is a web application which aspires to provide an intuitive and user-friendly method for collecting data from various sources about courses from restaurants to display the ideal menu to the users in a concise manner.

There are many students, that have different dietary requirements and on a daily basis struggle to find a place that suits their needs. The source of these need may be cultural, social or religious, among others, but irrespective of their origin, this application aims to help them meet these.
