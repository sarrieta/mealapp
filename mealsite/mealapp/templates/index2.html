About Us

Mealdr is a web application which aspires to provide an intuitive and user-friendly method for collecting data from various sources about foods from restaurants and display it to the users.

Services

What We Do.

There are many people that have different dietary requirements and on a daily basis struggle to find a place that suits their needs. The source of these need may be cultural, social or religious, among others, but irrespective of their origin, this application aims to help them meet these.

Maps

The integrated map will display the locations of the nearby restaurants

Filtering

Make use of our keywords to filter out based on your preferences

Menu

Search returns the dishes available to the user from the menu

Directions

Makes use of our Geolocation services to show directions to the restaurants.
